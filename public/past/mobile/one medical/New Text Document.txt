A membership-based primary care app offering seamless access to in-office and virtual visits, prescription management, secure messaging, and health record syncing. Designed to provide comprehensive care for both body and mind.
Role: React Native / iOS Developer
Features & Challenges:

Developed functionalities including appointment scheduling, prescription refills, secure messaging, and health record access. Integrated 24/7 virtual care services and Apple Health syncing. Challenges included ensuring HIPAA compliance, optimizing performance across devices, and managing real-time data synchronization.
Tech Stack: 
React Native, Redux, TypeScript, Firebase (Firestore, Auth), REST APIs, Apple HealthKit, In-App Payments, Push Notifications
