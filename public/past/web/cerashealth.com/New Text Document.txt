Ceras Health
A healthcare platform offering remote patient monitoring and digital health solutions to improve clinical outcomes and patient engagement. It integrates wearable devices, dashboards, and care team tools.

Role: Full Stack Developer

Features & Challenges:
I developed patient and clinician dashboards with real-time health data visualization and alerts. I built secure RESTful APIs for device data ingestion and managed role-based access control across the platform. Ensuring HIPAA compliance and data security was a critical challenge throughout development. I also optimized backend services to support high data throughput from wearables.

Tech Stack: React, Node.js, Express, PostgreSQL, AWS, Docker, GraphQL
